About the Movie

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse (2018) is an animated superhero movie that follows the story of Miles Morales, a teenager who get bitten by a radioactive spider and gains spider-like abilities. The movie explores the concept of the "Spider-Verse," where multiple versions of Spider-Man from different dimensions come together to fight a common threat.

Main Characters

  • Miles Morales

    A teenage boy in New york city, figuring out he's identity, responsibilities in the world. Becomes he's universes Spider-Man.

  • Peter B. Parker

    An older, more experienced version of Spider-Man who becomes Mile's mentor.

  • Gwen Stacy

    Also known as Spider-Woman, she is a skilled and agile superhero from another dimension. Teams up with Miles Morales.
